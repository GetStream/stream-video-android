CallLifecycle

@Composable
fun CallLifecycle(call: Call, enableInPictureInPicture: Boolean = false, pipEnteringDuration: Long = 250)

Register a call lifecycle that leaves a call depending on lifecycles. The default behavior is like so:

  • call will be leaved if the lifecycle is onDestroyed.

  • call will be leaved if the lifecycle is onStop, and on the PIP mode.

Parameters

call

The call includes states and will be rendered with participants.

pipEnteringDuration

The duration requires to be engaged in Picture-In-Picture mode.
